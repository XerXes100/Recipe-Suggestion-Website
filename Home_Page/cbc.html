Cocoa and Blueberry Cream

Ingredients Required

  • 2 1/4 cups heavy (whipping) cream
  • 2 Tbsp. dark cocoa powder
  • 2 tsp. confectionerâ€™s sugar
  • 14 oz. blueberries

Step 1

Pour the cream into a mixing bowl and refrigerate for 1 hour.

Step 2

Five minutes before serving, beat the cream until stiff peaks are formed.

Step 3

Add the cocoa and sugar. Beat for an additional minute to mix well.

Step 4

Serve in ramekins with the blueberries.
