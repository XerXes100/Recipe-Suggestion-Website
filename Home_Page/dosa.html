Masala Dosa

Ingredients Required

  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Dosa batter
  • 1 tsp mustard
  • ½ tsp urad dal
  • ½ tsp chana dal
  • pinch hing
  • few curry leaves
  • 3 chilli (finely chopped)
  • 1 inch ginger (finely chopped)
  • 1 onion (sliced)
  • ¼ tsp turmeric
  • 4 potato (boiled & mashed)
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 3 tsp lemon juice
  • 2 tbsp coriander (finely chopped)

Step 1

Firstly, in a large kadai heat 2 tbsp oil. Add 1 tsp mustard, ½ tsp urad dal, ½ tsp chana dal, pinch hing and few curry leaves.

Step 2

Add 3 chilli, 1 inch ginger and saute slightly. Further, add 1 onion and saute until the onions shrink without turning brown. Now add ¼ tsp turmeric and saute slightly. Further add 4 potato, ½ tsp salt and mix well.

Step 3

Mash the potato well until it turns smooth. Add 3 tsp lemon juice and 2 tbsp coriander. Mix well.

Step 4

Add a ladleful of batter on hot tawa. Spread slightly thick to make bangalore style dosa. Spread some oil and butter uniformly.

Step 5

Place 2 tbsp of prepared aloo masala in the centre. Roast until the dosa turns golden brown and crisp. Scrape the sides of the dosa and fold half. Finally, masala dosa recipe is ready to serve.
